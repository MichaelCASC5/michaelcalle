A Love for Creation

I've been using Blender 3D for maybe 8 years by now. I've always chosen 3D software to be my means to build, since the possibilities really are endless. I've carried over this mentality to my love of programming, and I believe that these tools allow us to express ourselves in ways that would not be reasonable without technology.

I have also started combining my knowledge of programming and of 3D software to build simulations in Unity 3D, and my own 3D engine.

Music and Art

I'm been playing and writing music for about 9 years, it's a hobby of mine aside from tech and computers. I've also done musical programs and competitions over some summers.

If you hit a wrong note then make it right by what you play afterwards. - Joe Pass

Music, and even creative writing, gives me other ways to create things without coding and programming. These hobbies may be quite different, but regardless I take the mentality I developed to create art and I apply it to everything I'm passionate about. Including computer science!

What 3D art do I create?

Mainly, I really enjoy object modeling, which is why all my products are small scenes with a focus on a specfic thing/object. If you would like to see more of my Blender renders, check out my Instagram.
